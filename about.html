About the Festival

For the first time in New York City a film festival will be taking place "Open air" in Brooklyn Bridge Park, you will have the chance to see some critically-acclaimed movies from the most trendy director in Hollywood.

All movies will be show on a big IMAX screen with the wonderful view of downtown New York in the background.

We will also provide a wide variety of food and drinking stand all over the park, under the supervision of Executive Chef Gordon Ramsey.

The entrance of the festival will start at 5.30pm and the first movie will start at 6pm.
Please note that you cannot bring any drink nor food at the festival, be aware that our security staff might control your bag at the point of entry for safety reason.
